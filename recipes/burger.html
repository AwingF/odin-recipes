Description

A smash burger is a type of hamburger. According to the Food Network, "A smash burger is a thin beef patty cooked on a super-hot griddle. The burger is smashed down, usually with a spatula, to increase browning and craggy bits on the surface for extra flavor."[1] Popularized by Smashburger,[1] smash burgers have grown in popularity in recent years

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20)
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, ketchup, mustard
  • Optional: 1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat a griddle or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and form them into loose balls.
  3. Place the balls on the hot griddle and smash them down with a spatula to form thin patties.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, and cook for about 2-3 minutes until browned.
  5. Flip the patties, add a slice of cheese on top, and cook for another 1-2 minutes until the cheese melts.
  6. Toast the burger buns on the griddle until golden brown.
  7. Assemble the burgers with your choice of toppings and serve immediately.
  8. Enjoy your delicious smash burger!
