Pizza Margarita

Back to the main page...

Description

La pizza margarita (en italiano pizza Margherita) es una típica pizza napolitana elaborada con tomate, mozzarella (tradicionalmente se usa el Fior di latte), albahaca fresca, sal y aceite. Se trata de la pizza napolitana más popular, junto con la marinera.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza base
  • 200g mozzarella
  • 100g tomate triturado
  • Albahaca fresca
  • Sal y aceite de oliva al gusto

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (428°F).
  2. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the pizza base.
  3. Add the mozzarella cheese and fresh basil leaves.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt.
  5. Bake in the oven for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
  6. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, slice, and serve.
